Lesson 3: Parts of a Fraction

Let's take the next step and learn about the parts of a fraction.


WHAT ARE THE PARTS OF A FRACTION? GOOD QUESTION!

Fractions have two parts a top number and a bottom number.

The top number is called a numerator and the bottom part is called a denominator.
